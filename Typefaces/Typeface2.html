Heading 1

The Buenard typeface is a traditional serif font designed by Gustavo Ibarra for an Argentinian art publisher as part of his final project at the University of Buenos Aires. Its design subtly echoes the flow of human handwriting while maintaining the clean, consistent lines of a digital typeface. Ideal for articles, blogs, and quotes, Buenard brings a refined balance of elegance and warmth to any block of text.

font-family: "Buenard", serif;
font-size: 2.986rem;
font-weight: 400;
font-style: normal;
